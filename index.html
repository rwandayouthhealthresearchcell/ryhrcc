Empowering Youth to Lead the Future of Health Research

Welcome to the Rwanda Youth Health Research Cell organization, a platform dedicated to advancing youth-driven health research across diverse fields focusing health sector. Our mission is to nurture young talent and drive groundbreaking research that addresses global health challenges, from antimicrobial resistance to emerging infectious diseases.
Join us in shaping the future of health through research, collaboration, and innovation.

About Us

Rwanda Youth Health Research Cell is a youth-focused non-governmental organization committed to conducting health research that addresses the pressing health challenges of today and tomorrow. We bring together young professionals from a wide array of health-related fields, including biotechnology, microbiology, medical doctors, biomedical laboratory technicians, veterinary doctors, public health professionals, and IT specialists. Our goal is to equip young researchers with the skills and knowledge they need to conduct impactful research, critically analyze health challenges, and propose sustainable solutions.

Our Research Areas

  • Biotechnology and Microbiology: Investigating solutions to antimicrobial resistance and exploring novel biotechnological innovations.
  • Public Health: Addressing critical public health issues, including infectious and non-infectious diseases.
  • Pharmaceutical Research: Collaborating on projects to develop and test new medical treatments.
  • Veterinary Science: Researching zoonotic diseases and advancing veterinary practices.
  • Information Technology in Health: Applying IT solutions to streamline healthcare data management and research processes.
